About Sjc Grove

Sjc Grove is a dedicated portal for VTU students to access quality study materials, year-wise and branch-wise notes, previous year question papers, syllabus, and exam resources—totally free.

Our mission is to support students and educators by making academic resources accessible online, helping every learner perform their best and stay prepared for exams.

Whether you’re searching for notes, model papers, tutorials, or tools, VTU Circle is your one-stop solution. We update our content regularly and always strive for accuracy and relevance.
